Blizzard Beach

Blizzard Beach's theme is a ski resort that has melted into a watery wonderland. It is home to one of the world's tallest and fastest waterslides. The best attractions are the chairlift, the lazy river (cross county creek), the watercoasters, and summit plummit. If you're looking for a snack, there are various stands and quick-stop restaurants where you can grab a bite to eat!
