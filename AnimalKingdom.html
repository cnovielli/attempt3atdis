Animal Kingdom

Once you step foot into animal kingdom, you are surrounded by an explorational environment filled with the sounds of the wild. In Animal kingdom, you can visit real animals through their magnificent attractions. The best of which being Kilimanjaro Safari and Maharajah Jungle Trek. Non-live animal attractions include Avatar flight of passage, expedition everest, kali river rapids, DINOSAUR, it's tough to be a bug, and the tree of life.
