Epcot

Epcot is AMAZING! From delicious food the cultures of other countries, Epcot is meant to be explored all day. It includes Mexico, Norway, Germany, Italy, USA, Japan, Morocco, France, the United Kingdom, and Canada. The best attractions in Epcot are The Seas with Nemo and Friends, Soarin, Spaceship Earth, Mission, Space! and Frozen Ever After. Everywhere you go, you will find amazing food in all of the different "countries." The best thing to do is just eat a little bit of everything!

    Standouts:
  • Macaron Ice Cream Sandwhich in Frace
  • Fish and Chips in the United Kingdom
  • Chips and Salsa in Mexico
  • Pizza in Italy
  • Werther's Caramels in Germany
  • San Angel Inn Restaurante in Mexico
