Magic Kingdom

Magic Kingdom is the main theme park in Disney World. It includes Main Street, USA; Adventureland; Tomorrowland; Frontierland; and Fantasyland. It is also home to the most recognized landmark in Disney World: Cinderella's Castle. The best attractions in Magic Kingdom are Splash Mountain, Pirates of the Caribbean, Space Mountain, It's a small world, Haunted Mansion, Jungle Cruise, and the Enchanted Tiki Room. Make sure to check out the beautiful fireworks launched from Cinderella's Castle every night!
